## Medium Version (for Apple App Store - ~2000 chars)

**Welcome to WalkWise!**

Your personal guide to exploring cities on foot. WalkWise combines physical activity with discovery, making every walk an adventure.

**Key Features:**

• **Multiple Cities & Venues** - Choose from a variety of locations to explore
• **Guided Walking Tours** - Follow step-by-step navigation through curated routes
• **Real-Time Step Tracking** - Powered by AWTY Engine for accurate progress monitoring
• **Fun Facts** - Learn interesting information about each location as you walk
• **Beautiful Location Images** - See stunning photos when you arrive at each destination
• **Tour Resumption** - Pick up where you left off - your progress is automatically saved
• **Interactive Route Maps** - Visualize your journey and see where you're heading
• **Complete at Your Own Pace** - No time limits, explore when and how you want

WalkWise makes exploring new cities fun, active, and educational. Whether you're a local looking to rediscover your city or a visitor wanting to see the sights, WalkWise guides you on foot-powered adventures.

Start your walking tour today and discover the joy of exploring on foot!
